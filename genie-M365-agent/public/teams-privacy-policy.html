1. Scope & Roles

Depending on the deployment model, we may act as a controller (determining purposes/means of processing) or a processor (processing on behalf of your organization). Where we act as a processor, your organization is the controller and our processing is subject to their instructions.

2. Data We Collect

Categories of personal data we may process include:

  • Account & Identity: name, email, tenant ID, user ID, role/group membership.
  • Usage & Telemetry: logs, events, diagnostics, interaction metadata, device type.
  • Content: messages, files, prompts, or records you submit within the App (collectively, “User Content”).
  • Support: information you provide to our support team.

Sensitive data: do not submit special categories of data (e.g., health, biometric, precise geolocation) unless explicitly permitted by your organization and applicable law.

3. Sources of Personal Data

  • Directly from you when you install or use the App.
  • From Microsoft via Microsoft Teams APIs as authorized by your tenant.
  • From your organization (e.g., identity provider groups, permissions, directories).
  • From our service providers who assist in delivering the App.

4. Purposes & Legal Bases

We use personal data for the following purposes, under legal bases that may include contract, legitimate interests, consent, and compliance with legal obligations:

  • Providing and operating the App’s features within Microsoft Teams;
  • Authentication, authorization, auditing, and preventing misuse;
  • Improving performance, reliability, and user experience;
  • Providing customer support and troubleshooting;
  • Compliance with applicable laws, investigations, and enforcement.

5. How We Process Data

Processing activities may include collection, recording, structuring, storage, adaptation, retrieval, transmission, restriction, and deletion of data. We apply appropriate safeguards and access controls based on the principle of least privilege.

6. Retention
